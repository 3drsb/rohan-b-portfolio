My Games

Game 1: Gravity Shift

Description: The player needs to navigate through levels, using environmental physics (e.g., moving platforms, jumping on bouncing objects, or using gravity to move through areas).

Instructions: Use arrow keys or WASD to move. Press space to jump. Interact with gravity switches to change the direction of gravity and survive obstacles.

Graphics: Basic 3D models with simple textures. The environment includes platforms that move with physics and obstacles that challenge the player to think about how they interact with gravity.

Play on itch.io

Game 2: Shape Pusher

Description: Interact with various shapes in a specific sequence to unlock new areas.

Instructions: Move the character using the arrow keys. Push shapes with in the zone.

Graphics: A simple, top-down view with a limited colour palette for easy navigation. Emphasis on clean UI elements and visual cues for interaction.

Play on itch.io

Game 3: Run N Jump

Description: An endless runner game where the character moves forward, and the player needs to jump or dodge obstacles to survive as long as possible.

Instructions: Press space to jump, or press down to slide under obstacles. The goal is to avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible.

Graphics: Simple, stylized graphics with an emphasis on slow-moving environments.

Play on itch.io
