My Games

Game 1: Ball Bounce Challenge

Description: watch as bouncing balls collide with each other in a dynamic, ever-changing environment. The goal is to observe the action, enjoy the unpredictable bounce dynamics, and see how long the balls continue to bounce around.

Instructions: Watch as the balls bounce off walls and collide with each other.

Graphics: Simple green border and a football bouncing with the camera on a 2.5D view.

Play on itch.io

Game 2: Hit500Game - RNG

Description: "Hit 500" is a simple game where you click a button to generate a random 3-digit number. Your goal is to hit exactly 500. The game provides feedback on how close you are with messages like "Hot," "Warm," or "Cold."

Instructions: Click the "Generate Number" button to produce a random 3-digit number and look at the results/feedback.

Graphics: A simple camera facing the UI.

Play on itch.io

Game 3: Run N Jump

Description: An endless runner game where the character moves forward, and the player needs to jump or dodge obstacles to survive as long as possible.

Instructions: Press space to jump, or press down to slide under obstacles. The goal is to avoid obstacles and survive as long as possible.

Graphics: Simple, stylized graphics with an emphasis on slow-moving environments.

Play on itch.io
